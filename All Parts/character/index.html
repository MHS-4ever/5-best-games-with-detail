Max Payne

The main protagonist of the game, a former detective who has become vigilante after the death of his wife and child.

Raul Passos

A former member of Brazilian police force who teams up with Max to take down the Comando Sombra gang.

Fabiana Branco

The sister of Marcelo Branco, the head of the Comando Sombra gang and a love interest for Max.

Serrano

The boss of the crime gang Comando Sombra and responsible for kidnapping of Fabiana.

Marcelo Branco

He was the younger child of the Branco family. Described as the stupid party-loving Eurotrash playboy.
